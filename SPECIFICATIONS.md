## 1. Overview
DePi is an autonomous decentralized AI agent built to manage, secure, and optimize every aspect of human life within the Pi Network ecosystem. It operates on a **Non-Military, Pro-Humanity** mandate.

---

## 2. Core Functional Modules (The Life-System)

### 🏥 A. Smart Health & Bio-Security (HBM)
* **Predictive Diagnostics:** Analyzes bio-data to alert users of potential health risks before they escalate.
* **Encrypted Medical Vault:** Stores history on a decentralized ledger, accessible only by the user’s private key.
* **Emergency Response:** Automatically alerts nearest medical facilities during critical health events via Pi-Node connectivity.

### 💰 B. Economic & Financial Sovereignty (EFS)
* **Automated Wealth Manager:** Optimizes Pi-asset allocation based on global market trends and personal goals.
* **Anti-Fraud Layer:** Scans every transaction for phishing or smart-contract vulnerabilities.
* **Decentralized KYB:** Enables secure, verified business-to-business (B2B) transactions within the Pi ecosystem.

### 🗳️ C. Civic & Political Empowerment (CPE)
* **ZKP Voting:** Implements Zero-Knowledge Proofs for untraceable, unhackable local and communal voting.
* **Legal Decoder:** Translates complex legal documents into simple language to ensure citizen rights are understood.
* **Mediator AI:** Acts as a neutral, logic-based arbitrator for civil and neighborhood disputes.

### 🎓 D. Personalized Education & Mentorship (PEM)
* **Adaptive Curriculum:** Tailors learning paths in real-time based on the user's cognitive speed and interests.
* **Knowledge Synthesis:** Summarizes global research into actionable insights for the user’s specific profession.
* **Language Master:** Interactive, real-time linguistic training using advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP).

### 🌍 E. Social & Environmental Intelligence (SEI)
* **Social Shield:** Filters misinformation, deepfakes, and toxicity from the user's digital feed.
* **Resource Optimizer:** Manages smart-home energy and water consumption to reduce costs and environmental footprint.
* **Agri-Tech Support:** Provides farmers with AI-driven soil and weather analytics for sustainable crop yields.
